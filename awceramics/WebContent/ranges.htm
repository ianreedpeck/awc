All Jewellery Ranges


I have always been a keen photographer and used to print my own black and white prints. Now I incorporate my own photographs into my handmade ceramic jewellery. The result, a stylish, distinctive, and highly original product. My subject matter is eclectic and wide ranging and I have favourite themes: all things urban - architectural details, drains, blocks of flats, graffiti, but also mosaic, stained glass and patterns from the 50s, 60s and 70s.
