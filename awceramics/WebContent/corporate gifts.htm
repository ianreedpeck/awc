Corporate Gifts

"We asked Allison to produce a line as a small momento for staff who had completed the transformation of the athletes village into new homes and we were all delighted with the the result. This was a modest thank you to staff who had worked hard to deliver a successful outcome; the range and flexibility of the products was excellent and meant that staff had a choice - all in all a very worthwhile and positive experience."

Gerry Murphy, Chief Operating Officer, ODA (Olympic Development Authority)

Imagine giving your customers, workforce, valued suppliers and/or distributors a gift that they would actually like and want to wear, and that was British made.

Allison Wiffens ceramic jewellery pieces are available for bulk purchase as corporate gifts  prices are available on application.

Alternatively if you want in excess of 30 items and have time in hand (approx one to two months?) why not develop a completely unique gift based on an image unique to you. This could be an image or images you supply (subject to suitability) or Allison could take a photograph relating to your building or some aspect of your product or service.

Allison has provided corporate gifts for several promotions companies as well as The Office of the Mayor of Lewisham, The Olympic Development Authority, International architects Bennoy, and Chubb Insurance.

Here are some of the bespoke pieces developed for the ODA (Olympic Development Authority) based on photographs Allison took of the East Village project  developed out of the 2012 Olympic Village in Stratford in East London.
