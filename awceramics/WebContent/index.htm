Allison Wiffen Ceramics

Miniature works of art.

Allison is a modern day miniaturist, straddling the worlds of both art and craft. Trained in ceramics and the visual arts, but always a keen photographer, her work combines the two skills. The result, a stylish, distinctive and highly original product. Her subject matter is eclectic and wide ranging but she has her favourite themes: all things urban - architectural details, drains, blocks of flats, graffiti, but also mosaic, stained glass and patterns from the 50s 60s and 70s. Her pieces are all handmade of the finest white earthenware.
