Graffiti Pendants

I am a big fan of a good piece of graffiti, and I marvel that people can create some wonderful works of art, happy in the knowledge that they are temporary. These pieces of graffiti were local to where I lived (Deptford, London SE8). I say were because not only has each piece been erased but because the wall they occupied along the side of the then undeveloped Seager Distillery building is no more.
