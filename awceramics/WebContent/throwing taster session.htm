Fancy having a go on the potter's wheel?:

If you have always wanted a go on the wheel, why not book a throwing taster session for you and a friend or two. It can even be a surprise present for someone.

I have two wheels, so two is a good number, but it can be as many as four people if you are prepared to take turns. I am flexible about day, date and time.

The costs are as follows:

1 person £100
2 people £150
3 people £185
4 people £210

The session usually runs for about two hours and I will fire and glaze one of the pots for each person which can be collected from the studio about 4 weeks later. Happy to post to you but there will be a £5 charge to cover postage.
