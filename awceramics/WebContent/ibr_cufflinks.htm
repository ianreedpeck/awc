Iconic Bristol Cufflinks

When I went to photograph Bristol I did not know it well, my memory of it from a previous visit being of the suspension bridge. And again the suspension bridge delighted me in every way: its setting, its robust and beautiful form. What I didnt know about was the wonderful are of Stokes Croft where a spend a good few hours wandering around, checking out the shops, soaking up the atmosphere and taking photographs of the great graffiti. So my Bristol range has three graffiti cufflinks in it  reflecting this vibrant art form that Bristol has made its own. Read my blog about Bristol and see other photographs from my visit.
