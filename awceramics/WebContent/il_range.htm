Iconic London Range

I came to London with the intention to stay a couple of years  that was thirty years ago! So I do feel that is my city. There are many more images that could be added to this range, but I have tried to keep it to those things modern and traditional that for me are iconic. The exception is probably Thetis  but she is just lovely, and as a ceramist, the V & A is for me a very special place.

I also have a range based on drains, railings, station roofs and walls that I have photographed in London which you might also want to check out.
