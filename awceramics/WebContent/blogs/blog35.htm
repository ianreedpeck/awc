It’s show time!

It seems ages since I’ve done a show of any kind – in fact the last was the ACE convention in February. Now I have Pulse in a week’s time followed by the Cockpit summer Open Studio.

I have no idea how successful Pulse will be and so I approach it with an open mind. It’s difficult times for the small independent gift shops and galleries, so they might be doing the rounds but not actually buying – and who can blame them .

The summer Open Studio at Cockpit will almost certainly be fun – it always is. I’ve been playing with images from playing cards, I have new work in development, and also lots of samples to offload – so there will be bargains as well as my standard ranges. And on the Friday evening we are welcoming people with a free glass of cava to get them in the party mood . . . and who knows I may have a glass or two myself!
