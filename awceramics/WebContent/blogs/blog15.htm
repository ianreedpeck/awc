Opening the kiln to see new work...

This is the most exciting part of what I do. And joy of joys I am about to do it! The pieces inside are for the Dulwich Picture Gallery, St Paul's Cathedral and also the Iconic Scotland pieces which I hope to sell online and take to Top Drawer in the autumn. Will they be beautiful/ok/rubbish – I just don’t know. In some ways I have to try not to get too excited as that inevitably leads to disappointment. It in fact takes a while to soak in the results and to decide if you really like it or not.

Sometimes the beauty of a thing grows on us and sometimes the gaudy attraction of something palls – as with people . . .
