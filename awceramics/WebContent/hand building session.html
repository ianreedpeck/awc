Hand Building Session




Play with clay for a couple of hours with friends or your kids. This is a two hour session modelling animals and/or making a small plaque. It’s about playing together, laughing together and having fun.

I will biscuit fire and then glaze the results so you have a memento of your fun time together. I use a blue and/or a green glaze on pieces.

Alternatively you can have a second shorter session – probably about an hour – in which you can apply various colours to your piece which I will then go on to glaze and fire.

As this is a group activity I usually expect you to book as a group of between 4 and 8 people.

Prices include clay, blue/green glazes and 2 x firings for a minimum of 4 people:

4 Adults £200 - each additional adult is an extra £50
2 Adults and 2 children £150 - each additional child is an extra £25

Hand colouring


If as a group you decide to hand colour your pieces once they have been biscuit fired, approximately 3-4 weeks after the original making session, this would add £30 per adult and £20 per child.

The pieces would then be glazed with a clear glaze.
